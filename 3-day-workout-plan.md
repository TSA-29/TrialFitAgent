# 3-Day Gym Workout Plan

## Overview
- **Frequency**: 3 days/week (e.g., Mon-Wed-Fri)
- **Split**: Upper/Lower/Upper
- **Focus**: Compound movements, progressive overload
- **Rest**: 60-90 seconds between sets

---

## Day 1: Upper Body (Push Focus)

### Chest
- Barbell Bench Press: 4x8-10
- Incline Dumbbell Press: 3x10-12
- Cable Flyes: 3x12-15

### Shoulders
- Overhead Press (Barbell or Dumbbell): 4x8-10
- Lateral Raises: 3x12-15
- Face Pulls: 3x15-20

### Triceps
- Tricep Pushdowns: 3x10-12
- Overhead Tricep Extension: 3x10-12
- Dips: 3x8-10

---

## Day 2: Lower Body

### Quadriceps
- Barbell Squats: 4x6-8
- Leg Press: 3x10-12
- Leg Extensions: 3x12-15

### Hamstrings/Glutes
- Romanian Deadlifts: 4x8-10
- Leg Curls: 3x10-12
- Hip Thrusts: 3x10-12

### Calves
- Standing Calf Raises: 4x12-15
- Seated Calf Raises: 3x15-20

---

## Day 3: Upper Body (Pull Focus)

### Back
- Pull-Ups (or Lat Pulldowns): 4x6-10
- Bent-Over Rows: 4x8-10
- Seated Cable Rows: 3x10-12

### Biceps
- Barbell Curls: 3x8-10
- Hammer Curls: 3x10-12
- Preacher Curls: 3x10-12

### Rear Delts/Upper Back
- Reverse Flyes: 3x12-15
- Shrugs: 3x12-15

---

## Notes
- Warm up with 5-10 minutes of cardio and dynamic stretches.
- Increase weight when you can complete all reps with good form.
- Track your workouts to monitor progress.
- Stay hydrated and fuel properly around training.
